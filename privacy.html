Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026  ·  Questions? abdulrahmannisar.dev@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy describes how Capshot AI: Photo Captions ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play. Capshot AI is developed and operated by Abdulrahman Nisar. By using Capshot AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Capshot AI does not require account creation, and we do not collect your name, email address, or other registration information to use the App. We do, however, collect the following categories of information as part of the App's core functionality and through third-party services integrated into the App.

1.1 Photos and Images You Submit

When you use Capshot AI to generate a caption, the photo or image you select is sent to Replicate (a third-party AI hosting platform), which runs an OpenAI GPT (nano) model to analyze the image and generate a caption suggestion. The result is then returned to the App and displayed to you.

We do not store copies of your photos on our own servers. Replicate and OpenAI may temporarily process the image as part of generating a response, according to their own data handling and retention practices.

Do not submit photos containing sensitive personal information about yourself or others (e.g., images revealing health conditions, identity documents, or similarly sensitive content) unless you are comfortable with that data being processed by Replicate and OpenAI.

1.2 Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)

Capshot AI displays advertisements through Google AdMob. To serve ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically collects certain information, which may include:

  • Advertising ID (and/or other device/account identifiers)
  • IP address
  • Device information (device model, operating system version, language settings)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address or, if permitted, device location)
  • User interactions with ads (clicks, impressions, viewability)

This data is collected and used by Google and its advertising partners for purposes including ad personalization, ad performance measurement, and fraud prevention. Data is encrypted in transit.

1.3 Automatically Collected Technical Data

Capshot AI does not use a dedicated crash-reporting or analytics SDK. However, the Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) SDK automatically collects certain diagnostic and device information as part of its normal operation, including device identifiers, general diagnostic information related to ad serving and app stability, and basic usage signals tied to ad display.

This data is collected by Google as part of the AdMob SDK described in Section 1.2, and is not shared with any additional third party beyond Google for this purpose.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Generate the AI-powered captions you request
  • Display advertisements through AdMob and measure ad performance
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations, where applicable

We do not sell your personal data.

3. How Information Is Shared

We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Replicate and OpenAI, solely to process the images you submit and generate captions
  • With Google AdMob and its advertising partners, as described in Section 1.2, for the purpose of serving and measuring ads
  • If required by law, such as in response to a valid legal request, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Developer, our users, or others

We do not share your photos or any personal data with any party for marketing purposes outside of what is described above.

4. Data Retention

Photos submitted for caption generation are not retained on our servers after the caption is generated and returned to you. Any temporary processing by the third-party AI provider is governed by that provider's own retention practices.

Advertising and diagnostic data collected via third-party SDKs (such as AdMob) is retained according to those providers' own retention policies. Since Capshot AI does not require an account, there is no persistent user profile tied to your identity that we retain.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (such as the GDPR in the EU/EEA or the CCPA in California), including the right to:

  • Know what data has been collected about you
  • Request access to or a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

Since Capshot AI does not require account creation and does not store your photos after caption generation, there is generally no personal data tied to an account for us to delete. If you have specific concerns about data collected via AdMob or our AI provider, you can:

  • Adjust your device's advertising ID settings or opt out of ad personalization via Google Ad Settings
  • Contact us directly at abdulrahmannisar.dev@gmail.com with any data-related request

6. Children's Privacy

Capshot AI is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the App and provided us with information, please contact us at abdulrahmannisar.dev@gmail.com so we can address it.

7. Data Security

While we take reasonable steps to protect information processed through the App, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
